Common questions

Will this get me into business class?+

Not directly, and we want to be upfront about that. Capital One Shopping won't upgrade your seat. What it does is give you real cashback on hotels, rental cars, and activities, which can meaningfully offset the total cost of your trip. A lot of travelers put those savings toward better accommodations or experiences they otherwise wouldn't splurge on.

Is it really free?+

Yes, completely. Capital One Shopping is free to use and always will be. No premium tier, no hidden fees, no credit card required. Capital One earns a small commission from merchants when it saves you money, so everyone wins.

Do I need a Capital One credit card?+

No. Capital One Shopping works for anyone with a browser, regardless of what bank or credit cards you use. You don't need to be a Capital One customer at all.

Why does my booking show as "ineligible" at first?+

This is completely normal for travel purchases. The status switches to "pending" once your trip completes and the vendor confirms. Your rewards are still being tracked. Don't cancel thinking it didn't work.

How do I redeem my rewards?+

Rewards are redeemed as gift cards including Marriott, Hotels.com, and airline gift cards, so you can put them right back into future travel. Standard offers cap at $250 per transaction; some targeted email offers go up to $5,000.
